Privacy Policy

App Developer Tycoon  ·  Last updated: January 2025

📋 Short version: App Developer Tycoon does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Who We Are

App Developer Tycoon is developed and published by PixelQuiz, an independent game studio. This privacy policy explains how our app handles your information.

2. Data We Collect

We collect no personal data whatsoever.

App Developer Tycoon does not:

  • Collect your name, email address, or any identifying information
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts, camera, microphone, or files
  • Use analytics services or third-party SDKs that collect data
  • Serve advertisements or share data with advertisers
  • Require an account or login
  • Connect to any PixelQuiz servers

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The app uses your device's local storage (localStorage) to save:

  • Your game progress (save file)
  • Your Hall of Fame records from previous runs

This data exists only on your device. It is never transmitted anywhere. You can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app or using the "Reset Save" option within the game.

4. Internet Access

The app requests internet permission solely to load fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) — the Orbitron and Rajdhani typefaces used in the game's UI. No game data is sent during this request. If you play offline, the app will fall back to system fonts.

We do not control Google Fonts' own data practices. Their privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Children's Privacy

App Developer Tycoon does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. As we collect no data at all, the app is safe for all ages from a data perspective.

6. Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party advertising networks, analytics platforms, crash reporting services, or social media SDKs.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the new version will be published at this URL with an updated date. We will not introduce data collection without clearly notifying users through an app update.
